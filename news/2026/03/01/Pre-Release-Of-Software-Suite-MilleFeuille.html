Pre-Release-Of-Software-Suite-MilleFeuille

Mar 1, 2026

The Millefeuille ecosystem is a software suite with a strong emphasis on cardiac microstructure that include:

  • Millefeuille extractor (done)

  • Millefeuille calculator (done)

  • Millefeuille segmentator (on going)

  • Millefeuille predictor (starting)

Millefeuille Services, a private (to be open source after publication) branch focused on the study of structural heterogeneities associated with cardiac arrhythmia.
