Charlotte Demeret is doing her master 2 internship over the spring-summer 2026 period at Liryc. In collaboration with Jairo Rodrigez Padilla (INRIA) and Julie Magat (Université de Bordeaux), she will develop new high-resolution MRI acquisition and image processing tools for the creation of new digital electrophysiology models for the treatment of cardiac arrhythmias. We welcome her to Bordeaux.
