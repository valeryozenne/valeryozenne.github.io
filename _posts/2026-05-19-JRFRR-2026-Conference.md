The annual conference of the "Journées du réseau français de recherche reproductible" [RJRFRR](https://jrfrr-2026.sciencesconf.org/?lang=fr) was in Bordeaux. A focus was done on the transactional package manager GNU Guix. We thanks the organiser for the nice practical course on [grid'5000 computers](https://www.grid5000.fr/w/Grid5000:Home).
