Nino Avetikovi and Dr. Manon Desclides are presenting their work at [RITS2026](https://rits2026.sciencesconf.org/) during the session "Innovations technologiques en santé 2".

* Magnetic resonance thermometry validation of bioheat transfer model for lesion size prediction in liver tumor microwave ablation procedures by Nino Avetikovi.

* Investigating the thermal effect of photodynamic therapy for glioblastoma treatment by Manon Desclides.
